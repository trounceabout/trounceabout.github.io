Hi, I'm Cody

I use design to make experiences better for people.

During my career as a designer, I’ve gone from graphic design to web design to motion design, and back again. Experiencing such a wide variety of design disciplines has given me a broad understanding of what design is, and how to use it to make better experiences.

Over the course of my time being a designer, I’ve had the opportunity to work with many tools and systems to create digital assets and interfaces. I have experience with Figma, Adobe Illustrator and After Effects, Sketch, Asana, Jira, Notion, HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript.

Looking for me elsewhere? Find me on Github, Dribbble, LinkedIn, and Twitter.

Web Design

Palmetto College

One of the projects that I’ve wrapped up most recently was a redesign of Palmetto College. While I had to stick with all the existing content, I completely restructured this section of the site to greatly lessen the number of pages. I also did a complete rework of the UI to make it feel more modern.

Army ROTC

This project required me to create a new section of the site for our ROTC program from scratch. I had to adapt content from another university to the new design, and used it as an opportunity to experiment by implementing Tailwind CSS for the first time in an effort to speed up the development process.

Transfers

For this project, I worked with our Transfer Admissions team to create a centralized page for all transfer information. This meant reviewing existing pages to cobble together content, and then creating a page versatile enough to house that content on. We’ve also added several portions since launch that helped illustrate the power of the modular structure.
